Yapping

Your voice transcribed to text in any app on your Mac.

Hold Fn key to speak, release to transcribe. Yapping transcribes your speech and pastes it right where your cursor is — ready to hit Enter. That's it.

  • • Configurable global hotkey for push-to-talk
  • • Multiple downloadable Whisper models (tiny, base, large-v3 turbo)
  • • Language selection — auto-detect or choose manually
  • • Launch at login
