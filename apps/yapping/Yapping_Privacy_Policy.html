Privacy Policy — Yapping

Last updated: February 7, 2026

Developer: Codexbit Software SRL

Website: https://codexbit.com

App: Yapping for macOS

The Short Version

Yapping processes everything locally on your Mac. We don’t collect, store, transmit, or have access to any of your data. We have no servers, no accounts, no analytics, and no way to see anything you do with the app.

1. Data Collection

We collect no data. Specifically:

  • No personal information — Yapping does not ask for, collect, or store your name, email address, or any other personal identifier.
  • No account required — There is no sign-up, login, or user account system.
  • No analytics or telemetry — Yapping does not include any analytics frameworks, crash reporting services, or usage tracking of any kind.
  • No advertising — Yapping contains no ads and no advertising SDKs.

2. Audio Data

Yapping uses your Mac’s microphone to capture audio for speech-to-text transcription. This audio is:

  • Processed entirely on your device using local Whisper speech recognition models.
  • Never transmitted to any server, cloud service, or third party.
  • Never stored on disk — audio is held in memory only during the active recording and transcription process, then discarded.
  • Never accessible to us — we have no mechanism to access, retrieve, or listen to your recordings.

3. Transcription Output

The text produced by transcription is pasted directly into your active application via macOS system APIs. Yapping does not store, log, or transmit transcription results. Once the text is pasted, Yapping retains no copy of it.

4. Model Downloads

Yapping includes a bundled speech recognition model that works offline. Optionally, you may download additional Whisper models from within the app. These downloads are fetched directly from Hugging Face (https://huggingface.co), a third-party model hosting service. When you initiate a model download:

  • A network request is made to Hugging Face to download the model file.
  • No personal information or identifiers are sent with this request.
  • Downloaded models are stored locally on your Mac in your Application Support directory.

We encourage you to review Hugging Face’s Privacy Policy for information about how they handle network requests.

Apart from optional model downloads, Yapping requires no internet connection to function.

5. System Permissions

Yapping requests the following macOS permissions, each used for a specific and limited purpose:

Permission Purpose
Microphone Capture audio for local speech-to-text transcription
Input Monitoring Detect the global push-to-talk hotkey
Accessibility Paste transcribed text into your active application

These permissions are used solely for the described purposes. No data obtained through these permissions is collected, stored, or transmitted.

6. Local Storage

Yapping stores the following data locally on your Mac:

  • User preferences (selected model, hotkey configuration, language setting, launch-at-login preference) in macOS UserDefaults.
  • Downloaded model files in ~/Library/Application Support/Yapping/models/.

This data remains on your device and is never transmitted anywhere.

7. Third-Party Services

Yapping does not integrate with any third-party services for analytics, advertising, crash reporting, or data processing. The only third-party interaction is the optional model download from Hugging Face, described in Section 4.

8. Children’s Privacy

Yapping does not knowingly collect any information from anyone, including children under the age of 13. Since we collect no data at all, there is no children’s data to protect — but we want to be explicit about it.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

If we update this Privacy Policy, we will revise the “Last updated” date at the top. Since Yapping collects no data, we do not anticipate material changes, but we will post any updates on our website.
