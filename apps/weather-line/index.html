Your weather forecast in a simple infographic

Features

Lock screen Widget

  • • Rapid access without unlocking your phone
  • • Much more useful than just the current temp
  • • Trend line provides obvious context
  • • Collapse to save space

Hourly

  • • 48 hours of highly accurate weather data
  • • Individual hourly icons show condition and temperature
  • • Trend line provides obvious context
  • • Deep details for each hour when selected

Daily

  • • Glance at your week ahead (8 days)
  • • One vibrant line with daily high temperatures and conditions
  • • Gray line shows nightly low temperatures and conditions
  • • Day-of-month context at a glance

Monthly

  • • Perfect for planning travel and vacation
  • • Average high and low temperatures
  • • Average rainfall
  • • Deeper details for planning activities

Dark Sky Rain Prediction

  • • Predicts rain or snow down to the minute
  • • Clear text summary
  • • Beautiful precipitation intensity graph
  • • Exceptionally accurate predictions
